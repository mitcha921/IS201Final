Mitch Anderson was born on October 20, 1999 in Walnut Creek, California, where he lived in the same house until graduating high school in 2018. He is the youngest of four children and has an older brother, Colter, and two older sisters, Madi and Emma. He also has an adorable 6-month-old niece, Evi.

Mitch loves sports, spending time with friends and family, and music. He is currently a member of Brigham Young University's best mixed a capella group, Dynamite.

Most important to Mitch, however, is his faith. In July 2021, he returned from serving a two-year, full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Austria, Germany, and Switzerland.
